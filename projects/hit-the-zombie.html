OVERVIEW

Hit The Zombie is a fast-paced driving arcade game where players survive an endless zombie apocalypse by driving through abandoned highways, crushing zombies, collecting fuel, and upgrading their vehicles. The gameplay combines satisfying vehicle physics with resource management, creating a rewarding progression loop where every run contributes toward building a stronger vehicle for future attempts.


Inspired by arcade survival games, the experience focuses on maintaining momentum while balancing fuel consumption, avoiding obstacles, maximizing zombie eliminations, and pushing further with every attempt.

MY ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES

This was a solo project — I was responsible for designing and building the complete game, including:

  • Game Design: Designed the fast-paced arcade mechanics, combo scoring system, and difficulty scaling to reward skillful play.
  • Programming: Implemented the enemy spawn system with object pooling, scoring logic, and responsive touch controls optimized for mobile.
  • UI/UX: Built a clean, readable HUD showing score, combo multiplier, and time/wave remaining.
  • Optimization: Profiled and optimized the game to keep frame rate stable even with a high number of enemies and effects on screen.

CASE STUDY

The Challenge

Deliver a satisfying, high-intensity arcade experience on mobile, with controls responsive enough for split-second reactions.

The Approach

Used object pooling to keep spawn/despawn cheap at scale, and iterated heavily on touch input responsiveness and combo feedback through playtesting.

The Result

A tight, replayable arcade loop with responsive controls and a scoring system that motivates players to beat their previous high score.
